Zorg Valtapaz, the Theorist

"Theorist" is a vague term, but Zorg fits almost any definition, from creating fun, game-based conspiracy theories, theories for universes and new stories, and global domination alternative citizenship theories. Currently 15 years old, he's often provided his siblings Mxyl and Klenda with the meat that becomes the basis of a storyline. He has also contributed to rounded characters and story arc ideas, and is currently working on his own novel. Also a daring skier and an outstanding rock climber, his knowledge and creative skills are priceless.
