The Nutty Buddy and the Cute Thing

Choclo and Oob are the youngest two thanes of the Zoomlian Empire. Choclo, at age 13, loves to socialize, often coming up with ideas and talking with Oob about them. Not knowing the meaning of stage fright, he has taken a liking to speaking not only with friends but also to an audience. A creative mind at heart, Choclo specializes in crafts, making various paper constructions from armor to airplanes.

Oob, 11, is an avid fan of birdwatching, having stated that he wishes to see every bird in the world, and he has taken to identifying a variety of birds by both appearance and call. A fan of discovery and exploration, Oob is always willing to lend a hand and will often be the first to suggest going on exercizing trips like biking. With a love of games and an insatiable taste for garlic, these two are inseparable.
