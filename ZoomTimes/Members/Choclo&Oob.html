The Nutty Buddy and the Cute Thing
Choclo and Oob are the youngest two thanes of the Zoomlian Empire. Choclo, at age 12, loves to socialize, often coming up with ideas and talking with Oob about them. Oob, although he's gotten a lot taller since his birth 10 years ago, has not gotten less cute, developing a tendency to come up with random quotes that suit however he happens to feel. With a love of games and an insatiable taste for garlic, these two are inseparable.

Here are some quotes from the Delightful Duo:

  • Oob: When I trip, after the tripping sound stops, I can hear my blood cells wiggling around!
  • Choclo: Curious George is so... glorious.
  • Oob: Oh, hevvins! Why'd I ever join the Footsie Team? Oh, heavens.
