Klenda Valtapaz, Artist and Novelist
Klenda Valtapaz is a 18-year-old artist with a love of puns and writing. She's written several stories, including fan-fictions and junior novels, several of which are co-written by her brother Mxyl. Although she primarily enjoys drawing using a blend of pens, pencils and computer software, she is also proficient in oil painting, drawing experience from three college courses and her own experimentation. She currently contributes to the world of fandom as a member of the Bionicle Comedy Central, and runs her own Deviantart page.

In addition to art and writing, Klenda has a love of wood carving and humor.
