Klenda Valtapaz, Artist and Novelist

Klenda Valtapaz is a 20-year-old artist with a love of puns and writing. She’s written several stories, from fan-fictions to junior novels, some of which are co-written by her brother Mxyl. Although she primarily enjoys drawing, from pens and pencils to charcoal to digital software, she is also proficient in oil painting, drawing experience from several college courses, a year so far of art school and her own experimentation. She currently contributes to the world of fandom as a member of the BCC Crew, and runs her own Deviantart page documenting her art work.

In addition to art and writing, Klenda has a love of books and humor.
