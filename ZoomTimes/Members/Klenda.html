Klenda Valtapaz, Artist and Adventuress

Klenda Valtapaz is a 21-year-old artist with a love of puns and writing. She's written a variety of stories, from fan-fictions to junior novels, some of which are co-written with her brother Mxyl. Although she primarily enjoys drawing, from pens and pencils to charcoal to digital software, she is also proficient in oil painting, watercolor and even sculpture, having drawn experience from a number of college courses, over a year of a dedicated art school and her own experimentation. She currently contributes to the world of fandom as a member of the BCC Crew, and runs her own DeviantArt page and Instagram page documenting her art work.

In addition to art and writing, Klenda has a love of books (classic or modern) and humor.
