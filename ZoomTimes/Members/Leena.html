Leena Valtapaz, Comedy, Fandom and Video Editing Enthusiast

If you've read these bios in order, you've probably gotten the idea that there's no such thing as a generic, average Zoomlian. Leena is no exception to this rule. Within just a few years, the 15-year-old has run a comedy blog for over a year, quickly taught herself how to use a vast number of media programs, used those programs to create fan-based music videos, joined several fandom teams, and began experimenting in animation. In addition to music videos, she has branched into 2D animation, stop-motion and voice acting.
