Leena Valtapaz, Composer, Video Editor and Voice Actress

If you've read these bios in order, you've probably gotten the idea that there's no such thing as a generic, average Zoomlian. Leena is no exception to this rule. After running a year-long comedy blog, the 17-year-old has taught herself a vast number of multimedia programs and now runs an eclectic YouTube channel with videos that feature digital art, fan projects, comedies, and more. Since starting her channel, Leena has also branched into voice acting, performing an variety of roles for various fandom productions. She has also stepped into her own music production, which has landed her a role as a professional composer in multiple projects.
