Leena Valtapaz, Comedy, Fandom and Video Editing Enthusiast

If you've read these bios in order, you've probably gotten the idea that there's no such thing as a generic, average Zoomlian. Leena is no exception to this rule. Within just a few years, the 14-year-old has run a comedy blog for over a year, quickly taught herself how to use a vast number of media programs, and used those programs to create fan-based music videos that eventually got her and her sister Klenda into the fandom group, the BCC. She currently continues to make music videos, and is also creating her own audio books and productions.
